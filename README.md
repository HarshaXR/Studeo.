# Studeo

<div align="center">

### Your Academic Workspace.

Organize subjects, manage resources, take notes, stay focused, and work with AI — all from a single native desktop experience.

---

## Overview

Studeo is a modern Windows study application designed around a simple idea:

> Your academic life shouldn't be scattered across folders, browser tabs, PDFs, note-taking apps, timers, and AI chatbots.

Instead, everything lives in one workspace.

Subjects, files, notes, deadlines, study sessions, and AI-powered workflows are brought together into a single desktop application built specifically for studying.

---

## Features

### Subject Workspaces

Create and manage subjects with:

* Subject Name
* Course Code
* Custom Icons
* Custom Themes
* Subject Dashboards

Each subject acts as its own dedicated workspace.

---

### Local File Management

Studeo is local-first.

When resources are imported:

* PDFs
* Documents
* Images
* Study Materials

they are copied into Studeo's local storage structure and organized automatically.

No mandatory cloud storage.

No internet required.

Your files remain yours.

---

### PDF Viewer

Built-in PDF experience with:

* Fast document viewing
* Annotation support
* Reading progress tracking
* AI integration directly inside the viewer

Study without constantly switching applications.

---

### Notes

Create notes directly inside Studeo.

Current functionality includes:

* Rich text editing
* Subject-linked notes
* Automatic organization

Future versions will introduce advanced note-linking and knowledge management features.

Everything tied directly to your subjects.

## Studeo Stage

One of Studeo's core features.

Access:

* ChatGPT
* Gemini
* Additional providers planned

directly inside Studeo.

No browser switching.

Simply drag resources into the AI workspace.

Examples include:

* Concept Explanation
* Study Notes
* Revision Material
* Quiz Generation
* Assignment Assistance
* Exam Preparation

Save time.

Spend less time writing prompts and more time studying.

---

### Prompt Variables

Dynamic prompt variables allow workflows to automatically include:

---

## AI-Powered PDF Actions

Studeo also brings AI directly into the PDF viewing experience.

While reading a document:

1. Select an AI action.
2. Studeo extracts only the relevant page.
3. The content is sent to your chosen AI provider.
4. Responses appear directly inside the Studeo interface.

This creates the feeling of a native AI assistant built directly into your study materials.

Without forcing users to manually upload entire documents.
